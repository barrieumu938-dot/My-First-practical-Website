Club history

Real Madrid 'Club de futbol' was found on March 6, 1902 in Madrid spain. It began as a small football club created by a group of university students who loved the sport. Overtime it grew as one of the most sucessful and recognized football teams in the world. The club was originally called Madrid football club. In 1920, King Alfonso XIII gave them the title 'Real' which means "Royal". They started winning regional and nation championships and built a strong reputation in spain. In 1955, the club helped create the European cup, now known as the UEFA champions League.
